01/2023 - Hello there

I don't like the urge for a publicized life. I understand that
external gratification (such as would come from an audience) is
fleeting and cannot sustain you as well as validation from within.
But here I am. I guess that I enjoy creating things that other
people like, and I've found that writing is the easiest way for
me to do that. Therefore, I hope you find something here that
gives you joy or peace. Even better, I wish you love and happiness.

January 29th

I've been trying to be more positive recently.

It's true that I've got a couple things to be displeased with. For
example, I'm having a bit of job trouble. Been having, actually,
for a little while. Since June. Though my job search has been
admittedly on-and-off, I was not expecting it to take this long.

I do have a job currently. I'm able to pay bills and I'm only a
leech on my parents in terms of insurance and phone plan. I just
wish that I were moving forward in my life instead of being stuck
in this limbo between school and work. I'm not getting many projects
done. I'm not very motivated.
And it's fucking frigid outside.

*Author's note: I don't swear while speaking. Pretty much ever.
I try to avoid it in formal writing as well. I've always had the
philosophy that there's a better word out there than whatever four-
letter one comes to mind first. Here, though, I think I want to
relax that rule of mine. After all, this is an informal blog.
And I can do whatever the hell I want.

With all that said, things are going very well. I have a supportive
partner and friends, I'm in good health. Overall I've got nothing
to really moan about, and all that moaning I'm doing anyways is
taking up my valuable time. It's probably annoying the neighbors
as well.

So I'm switching things up. I started a playlist with more
positive music. I set up the downtime feature on my phone to keep
me focused. Instead of sitting at my desk at home, tapping away at
my computer like a gremlin, I went out to a coffee shop and tapped
away at my computer like a gremlin. I started a blog.

Spring is soon, and change is in the air - I can feel it.
